Frequency Asked Questions

Download all (Chrome/Brave Settings)

To avoid "Save As" prompts for every single file, disable the prompt in your browser settings:

Chrome: chrome://settings/downloads

Brave: Settings > Downloads > Turn off "Ask where to save each file before downloading"

Where are my files saved?

Files are organized automatically in your Downloads folder:

Downloads/Udemy Download/[Instructor]/[Course]/[Video].mp4
