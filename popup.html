Frequently Asked Questions

Avoid "Save As" prompts on every download

Disable the per-file prompt in your browser settings so downloads start automatically.

Chrome: chrome://settings/downloads

Brave: Settings › Downloads › turn off "Ask where to save each file"

Where do files get saved?

Files are organised automatically inside your Downloads folder:

Downloads / Udemy Download / [Instructor] / [Course] / [Video].mp4
